Interest in AI

Now, the question is why I'm interested in AI whoever does it. AI programming is based on commonly used algorithms, and the real fun is actually created in models based on the real data. The art of model creation for future predictions and getting the accuracy of upwards of 95% is really challenging task. And it is a big achievement for the people who are able to achieve that. AI also offers the solution to many problems for which otherwise it is very difficult or nearly impossible. Traditional functional programming doesn’t offer solutions to problems like image recognition, image classification, product recommendation, autonomous driving, etc.
